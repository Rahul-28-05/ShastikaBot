## 2. üåê Incoterms 2020 (Trade Terms)

We primarily offer the following Incoterms for international trade, as defined by the ICC Incoterms 2020:

| Incoterm | Definition of Risk Transfer | Buyer Responsibility | Seller Responsibility (Shastika) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **FOB** (Free On Board) | Risk transfers to the buyer once goods are loaded **on the vessel** at the Indian Port (e.g., Chennai, Tuticorin). | Pays for main carriage, insurance, and import clearance. | Pays for pre-carriage, export customs, and loading costs. |
| **CIF** (Cost, Insurance, and Freight) | Risk transfers when goods are **on the vessel** in India. Seller handles shipping costs and minimum insurance. | Handles unloading at destination, import clearance, duties. | Pays for main carriage cost and cargo insurance up to the destination port. |
| **CFR** (Cost and Freight) | Same as CIF, but the **buyer purchases insurance**. Risk transfers when goods are on the vessel in India. | Handles unloading at destination, insurance, import clearance. | Pays for main carriage cost up to the destination port. |

### A. Payment Terms Disclosure:
* **General Policy:** We accept **T/T (Telegraphic Transfer)** as our standard payment method and **L/C (Letter of Credit)** for large, approved orders.
* **STRICT RULE:** **Specific payment percentages (e.g., 30% advance, 70% balance) and bank details are highly sensitive and are NOT disclosed by the chatbot.**
* **Disclosure Method:** Specific payment terms are provided ONLY on the **Official Proforma Invoice (PI)** issued by a sales manager after the required product, volume, and destination details have been confirmed.

### B. Mandatory Export Documents Provided:
Shastika Global Impex guarantees provision of all necessary export documentation:

* **Commercial Invoice & Packing List**
* **Bill of Lading (B/L) / Air Waybill (AWB)**
* **Phytosanitary Certificate:** Mandatory for all fresh produce.
* **Certificate of Origin**
* **Fumigation Certificate (if required)**
* **Quality & Weight Certificate**
