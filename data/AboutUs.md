# Shastika Global Impex Private Limited - Company Overview

## Core Business and Expertise

**Shastika Global Impex Private Limited** is an **accredited exporter and supplier** specializing in a variety of fresh produce, with a core focus on coconuts. We maintain a quality-centric approach to muster a large client base in the global marketplace.

We supply a comprehensive range of quality coconuts, sourced from trusted names and suppliers. Our product line is inclusive of:

* **Brown Coconut** (Matured and Semi-Husked)
* **Tender Coconut** (Ideal for water consumption)
* **Green Coconut** (Young, for specific culinary uses)
