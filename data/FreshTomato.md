Tomatoes are sweet, juicy, and delicious, making them an excellent healthy choice.

### Key Nutritional Components (Per 150g Serving - Approx. One Cup)

A single serving of red, ripe, raw tomatoes (150 grams) is an outstanding source of essential nutrients:

* **Vitamins:** Good source of Vitamins **A, C, K, folate**, and **potassium**.
* **Minerals:** Provides **thiamin, niacin, vitamin B6, magnesium, phosphorus**, and **copper**.
* **Fiber:** Contains **2 grams of fiber** per serving (7% of the daily recommended amount).

### Health Advantages

* Tomatoes are naturally **low in sodium, saturated fat, cholesterol, and calories**.
* Their relatively **high water content** makes them a filling food.
* Eating plenty of fruits and vegetables, including tomatoes, confers protection against:
    * High blood pressure
    * High cholesterol
    * Strokes
    * Heart disease
