## ðŸ’– Nutritional and Health Benefits

Watermelon is sweet, hydrating, and refreshing, making it an excellent health-boosting food.

### Key Nutritional Components (Per 150g Serving - Approx. One Cup)

One serving of watermelon (150 grams) is packed with essential nutrients:

* **Excellent Sources:** Vitamin **C**, Vitamin **A**, and **Potassium**.
* **B Vitamins:** Contains **B1 (thiamin), B5 (pantothenic acid), and B6 (pyridoxine)**, which are essential for energy production and brain function.
* **Antioxidants:** Provides powerful antioxidants like **lycopene** and **beta-carotene**, which help reduce inflammation.

### Health Advantages

* **Hydration:** Watermelon is approximately **92% water**, making it incredibly hydrating.
* **Heart Health:** Naturally low in **sodium, calories, and cholesterol**, making it a heart-healthy choice.
* **Digestion:** Contains fiber, which supports digestion and promotes a feeling of fullness.
* **Disease Protection:** Consumption can help lower blood pressure and reduce the risk of chronic diseases like cancer and heart disease.
